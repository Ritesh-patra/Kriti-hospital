Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for all your eye and dental needs

Excellence Eye Care

Cataract

Advanced cataract surgery with premium IOLs. Micro-incision techniques for faster recovery and better vision outcomes.

Glaucoma

Early detection and treatment of glaucoma using advanced diagnostic equipment and laser therapy options.

Retina

Comprehensive retinal care including diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and vitreoretinal surgery.

Cornea & Refractive

LASIK, PRK, and corneal transplant procedures. Freedom from glasses with advanced refractive surgery.

Squint

Squint correction surgery for children and adults. Restore proper eye alignment and binocular vision.

Neuro-ophthalmology

Treatment of neurological eye disorders, optic nerve diseases, and vision problems related to brain conditions.

UVEA

Specialized treatment for uveitis and inflammatory eye diseases with immunosuppressive therapy.

Opticals

Premium eyewear collection, contact lenses, and specialized optical solutions for all vision needs.

Excellence Dental Care

Cleanings

Professional dental cleaning, scaling, polishing, and preventive oral hygiene maintenance programs.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement with titanium implants. Natural-looking and long-lasting solutions.

Crowns & Bridges

High-quality ceramic and zirconia crowns and bridges for damaged or missing teeth restoration.

Sedation Dentistry

Comfortable, anxiety-free dental treatments with conscious sedation and nitrous oxide options.
